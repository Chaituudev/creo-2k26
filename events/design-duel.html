Quick note

About

What this event is about

Design Duel gives you a product brief and asks for a quick UX flow with high-impact screens. Showcase clarity, contrast, and smart microcopy.

Team size

Solo or duo

Duration

70 minutes

Tools allowed

Figma/Adobe XD/Sketch on your own device.

Rules

Read before you play

  • Use only assets you create or provided in the brief.
  • Stock templates are not allowed; originality matters.
  • Submit a clickable prototype or annotated frames.
  • Present your rationale briefly during judging.
Rounds

Round structure

  • Round 1: Wireframe the core user flow.
  • Round 2: Polish key screens and present to judges.
Venue

Where to meet

Design Studio, Block F, projector available.

Reporting

Arrive 15 minutes early to get your brief.

What to carry

College ID, laptop/charger, and your design tool of choice.
