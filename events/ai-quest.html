About

What this event is about

AI Quest challenges you to craft effective prompts, validate outputs, and reason about ethical trade-offs. Showcase structured thinking and clarity when working with AI systems.

Team size

Solo or duo

Duration

70 minutes

Tools allowed

Provided AI tools, offline notes, and laptops.

Rules

Read before you play

  • Use only the provided AI access; no external accounts.
  • Document prompt iterations in your submission.
  • Clearly cite data sources if used in reasoning.
  • Offensive or harmful prompts lead to disqualification.
Rounds

Round structure

  • Round 1: Prompt quality and response accuracy.
  • Round 2: Build a mini workflow using chained prompts.
  • Round 3: Present ethical considerations and guardrails.
