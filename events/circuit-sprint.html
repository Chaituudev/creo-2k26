About

What this event is about

Circuit Sprint tests your ability to translate schematics into working hardware fast. Balance accuracy, neat wiring, and safe measurements under time pressure.

Team size

2 to 3 members

Duration

75 minutes

Tools allowed

Provided kits, multimeter, your own jumper kit.

Rules

Read before you play

  • Use provided components; external modules not allowed.
  • Follow lab safety; no live wiring without approval.
  • Markings on boards must remain intact for evaluation.
  • Submit a clear circuit diagram with your final build.
Rounds

Round structure

  • Round 1: Basic circuit assembly and verification.
  • Round 2: Debug a faulty circuit faster than rivals.
  • Round 3: Mini build with added constraints for finals.
Venue

Where to meet

Hardware Lab, Block C, benches pre-assigned.

Reporting

Report 30 minutes early for kit allocation.

What to carry

College ID and any personal jumper kits you prefer.

Registration

Sign up details

  • Teams register together; one lead shares all names.
  • Slots limited to available hardware sets.
  • Confirmation email/SMS required at check-in.
Prizes

Breakdown

  • Winner: ₹5,000
  • Runner-up: ₹3,000
  • Best wiring discipline mention.
