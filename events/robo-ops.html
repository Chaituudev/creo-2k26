About

What this event is about

Robo Ops puts your autonomous bot against a dynamic track. Balance sensor tuning, stable firmware, and quick pit-stop fixes to finish clean runs.

Team size

Up to 3 members

Duration

90 minutes including setup

Tools allowed

Your own bot, batteries, and repair toolkit.

Rules

Read before you play

  • Only autonomous navigation; manual control is disallowed.
  • Spare parts permitted but builds must meet size limits.
  • No metal spikes or damaging components on the bot.
  • Time penalties apply for touching or resetting the bot.
Rounds

Round structure

  • Round 1: Basic lap with standard obstacles.
  • Round 2: Dynamic obstacles and reduced visibility.
  • Round 3: Finals with randomised track sections.
Venue

Where to meet

Main Arena, Block D courtyard, test lane available.

Reporting

Arrive 30 minutes early for inspection.

What to carry

College ID, fully charged batteries, and repair kit.

Registration

Sign up details

  • Team of 3; lead registers all member details.
  • Bot dimensions and specs submitted during check-in.
  • Confirmation slip mandatory at entry.

  • Fastest lap mention.
