About

What this event is about

Data Decode challenges you to find patterns, clean noise, and communicate results. Combine analysis, visualization, and sharp narration to convince the judges.

Team size

Solo or duo

Duration

80 minutes

Tools allowed

Spreadsheets, Python/R, and your preferred viz tools.
